    Financial Wellness

    Financial Wellness involves the process of learning how to successfully manage financial expenses. Money plays a critical role in our lives, and it can impact your health as well as academic performance.

    You can foster your financial wellness by:

  • Starting with small changes that will add up over time (i.e. make your own coffee rather than buying it).
  • Utilizing resources to expand your budget like CalFresh and other student discounts.
  • Understanding your student loans.
  • Starting a savings account – add a small monthly automatic payment, if possible.
  • Identifying and addressing any financial problems before they start.
  • Planning ahead and setting budget goals.
  • Making a list or reviewing your bank account of all purchases in one week.
  • Writing a list before you go shopping.
  • Asking yourself “do I really need this?” before an unplanned purchase.

    • Why Financial Wellness is Important:

    Retirement Security

    Planning for retirement is a key component of financial wellness. Individuals who prioritize financial planning are more likely to enjoy a secure and comfortable retirement.

    Goal Achievement

    Financial wellness is essential for setting and achieving personal and financial goals. Whether it's buying a home, pursuing education, or traveling, financial stability supports the realization of aspirations.

    Freedom

    Financial wellness provides individuals with the freedom to make choices that align with their values and goals. It allows for flexibility in career decisions, lifestyle choices, and overall life trajectory.

    Generational Impact

    Financial wellness has a generational impact. Parents who are financially well can provide a stable and secure environment for their children, influencing their financial habits and attitudes.

    Long-Term Wealth Building

    Financial wellness is crucial for building long-term wealth. Through responsible budgeting, investing, and savings, individuals can accumulate wealth and create a foundation for future financial success.
